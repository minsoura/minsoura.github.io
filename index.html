About Reflecta

Reflecta is an AI-powered voice journal that helps you speak freely, organize your thoughts, understand your emotions, and turn insights into action. Through natural conversation, Reflecta listens, summarizes, and helps you grow mindfully each day.

Just talk — Reflecta transforms your voice into a clear, structured journal entry. No typing required.
